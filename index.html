Objective:
• Developed a dynamic safety stock optimization model for ASIC inventory at Teradyne, addressing volatility in demand patterns and inconsistent lead times.

Approach:
• Analyzed historical MRP and E&O data using linear regression to uncover demand trends and compute part-specific risk coefficients.

Solution:

Results:
• Reduced safety stock value by 60% unlocking $36 million in savings while maintaining high availability and supply chain resilience.

Impact:
• Replaced Teradyne’s traditional “2-year buffer” with a scalable, data-driven strategy — setting a new standard for precision inventory planning.
